No failures detected. The dataset is internally consistent under the v1 rubric. One templating artifact worth noting: mechgap-0268 says "the largest customer non-renewed, where those three clients were 41% of pipeline" -- singular antecedent paired with a plural follow-up. This is a template-stitching seam, not a rubric violation; the case still has both a named entity and quantified evidence in the because-clause.
